```ad-theorem
$f: A \subseteq \mathbb R^n \to \mathbb R^p$, $a \in \textrm{ac}A$.

$$ \lim_{x \to a} f(x) = L \iff \left [ \forall (x_n)_{n \in \mathbb N} \subseteq A, \, \lim_{n \to + \infty} x_n = a \implies \lim_{n \to +\infty} f(x_n) = L \right ]$$
```
