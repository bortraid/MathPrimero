### Contenido Principal

```ad-proposition
Si $f: A \subseteq \mathbb R^n \to \mathbb R^p$, $p>1$, $a \in \textrm{ac}A$, entonces
$$\lim_{x \to a} f(x) = L = (L_1, L_2, \dots, L_p) \iff \lim_{x \to a} f_i(x) = L_i, \quad i = 1, \dots, p.$$
```
