### Contenido principal

```ad-Formal
Sean $p = \sum_{i = 0}^\infty r_i x^i, q = \sum_{i = 0}^\infty s_i x^i \in R[x]$ dos polinomios. Entonces definimos su multiplicación como
$$pq = \sum_{i = 0}^\infty \left ( \sum_{j+k = i} r_js_k \right )x^i.$$
```
