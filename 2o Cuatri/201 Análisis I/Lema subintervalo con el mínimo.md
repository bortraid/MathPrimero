### Contenido Principal

**Fecha:** 2024-01-27, 16:52

Sean $a,b, \xi \in \mathbb R$ con $a < \xi < b$,
```ad-lemma
Sea $\delta := \min\{\xi - a, b - \xi\}$, entonces 
$$]\xi - \delta, \xi + \delta[\subset]a,b[$$
```


```ad-proof
Tendremos
$$a = \xi - (\xi - a) \le \xi - \delta < \xi + \delta \le \xi + (b-\xi) = b$$
```
